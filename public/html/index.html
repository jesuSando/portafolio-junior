About Me

I'm a junior full-stack developer passionate about technology, with some experience building web applications and digital experiences.

I started my path in programming at a young age, experimenting with HTML and CSS, and today I specialize mainly in Node.js. I'm motivated by solving complex problems and developing intuitive and efficient interfaces that enhance the user experience.

When I'm not coding, you can find me cooking, drawing or watching movies.

My goal is to grow into a professional capable of leading development teams and delivering impactful software solutions.
